Bedding Your Clients Can See the Difference In

When you board horses for other people, the bedding in each stall says something about your operation. Make sure it says the right thing.

Volume Pricing That Works

Pallet, semi-load, and full truckload pricing means the more you order, the more you save. We calculate freight to your door — no guessing, no surprises. Contact us to build a standing order that keeps your barn stocked without you thinking about it.

Consistent Supply, Every Time

Running out mid-month or switching brands stresses horses and your barn staff. We work with you on delivery timing so you always have what you need. Same product, same quality, every delivery — your horses never notice the transition because there isn't one.

Healthy Horses, Happy Boarders

Triple-screened bedding means the respirable dust that triggers airway problems simply isn't there. For a boarding operation, that's a real differentiator — healthier horses, fewer vet calls, and boarders who can see you're investing in their animal's wellbeing.

Pine Pellets

40 lb bags  ·  55 bags/pallet  ·  Semi = 20 pallets

The most economical choice for high-volume boarding operations. Pellets expand 4× on contact with moisture — less product used per stall, easy manure management, and very low dust output.

  • Most bags per pallet — better per-unit economics
  • 4× expansion — less product consumed
  • Superior absorption reduces odor
  • Semi-load: 20 pallets / 1,100 bags
