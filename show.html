Why Show Barns Choose Bluegrass

Your Standards Deserve Better Bedding

Show horses breathe, rest, and recover in their stalls. The bedding underneath them affects every one of those things.

Airway Health First

Respirable dust from low-quality bedding is the #1 cause of subclinical airway inflammation in sport horses. Our triple-screening process eliminates it at the source. A horse with clear airways performs the way you trained it to.

Show-Ring Presentation

Clean, bright bedding reflects the level of care that wins respect in any barn aisle. Our pine shavings and pellets stay fresher, dry faster, and look better — because the way your barn looks is part of your reputation.

Consistent from Home to Show

Switching bedding at a show facility is a stressor your horse doesn't need. We deliver to training barns, private facilities, and show venues so your horses are always on the same bedding — familiar, comfortable, and ready to go.

Every Discipline

One Bedding Standard Across Your Barn

Whether your barn runs one discipline or six, every horse under your roof deserves the same dust-free environment. Bluegrass Bedding works equally well for every style of riding — and we offer all three formats to match your bedding preference.

Dressage Hunter/Jumper Western Saddlebred Eventing Reining
Dressage

Collection and suppleness demand peak lung capacity. Dust-free bedding means your horse can breathe deeply through every movement — down centerline and beyond.

Hunter / Jumper

Jumping requires explosive power and recovery. Horses with compromised airways fatigue earlier and recover slower. Start every round with lungs that are clear and ready.

Western Performance

From reining spins to cutting, western horses rely on quick response and sustained effort. Clean bedding keeps their breathing unobstructed and their stalls tidy for long haul travel schedules.

Saddlebred & Gaited

Presentation is everything. Stalls that smell clean and look bright set the tone before your horse ever enters the ring. Macro Flake is a natural fit for deep bedding in show barn stalls.
