Pine Pellets

Most Popular

Kiln-dried pine compressed into dense pellets that expand up to 4× when wet — delivering a deep, soft, dust-free bed from a compact bag. Tight-clumping absorption means you pull only what's soiled, cutting waste and labor. Ideal for barns with rubber mats.
